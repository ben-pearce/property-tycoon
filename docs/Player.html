Methods

charge(amount, depositInto, cbnullable)

Charges a player a specified amount of money and deposits into a specified location. If player can afford to pay the amount will be withdrawn from their account and callback will be invoked. If a player cannot afford to pay an amount this function will ask them to sell off assets until they can. If a player still cannot afford to pay an amount after selling assets they will be forced to retire.
Parameters:
Name Type Attributes Default Description
amount integer The amount the player must pay.
depositInto Player | Bank | Parking Where the amount must be deposited into.
cb Player~debtPaidCallback <nullable>
 null The callback to invoke after any payment and sale of assets.
